What We Do

Arc Ciel Africa provides innovative drone and energy solutions designed to transform industries and promote sustainable growth. Our expert services empower organizations to harness technology for efficiency, safety, and a greener future.

Drone Training and Certifications (UTO)

We provide certified drone operations for industries including agriculture, energy, and conservation. Our ROC ensures compliance with all necessary regulations, while we help external businesses with their drone operations setup.

Drone Operations Consultation

Our expert consultation services guide companies through the integration of drone technology, offering full support in building and managing in-house drone teams. We also provide operational support to ensure compliance with aviation regulations and the safe deployment of drones across various sectors.

Drone Maintenance

We offer a full suite of drone maintenance services, from routine inspections to optimization and repair. With our maintenance organization, you can ensure your drones remain in peak operational condition.

Drone Importation and Reselling

We import and resell high-quality drones to meet the needs of various sectors. Whether you’re an industrial user or a hobbyist, we ensure access to the best technology available.

Solar System Sizing and Plant Inspections

Our drone-based solar plant inspections and solar system sizing services optimize energy use and identify inefficiencies. We help businesses maximize their return on investment while contributing to greener energy solutions.

Robotics and Coding Classes for Kids

: We engage with schools to offer hands-on robotics and coding workshops that introduce students to future STEM careers. Through interactive sessions, we inspire students to embrace technology and innovation.
