Who We Are

Arc Ciel Africa is a pioneering technology company dedicated to advancing industries and communities across Africa through innovative drone solutions and sustainable energy practices. With a focus on precision, efficiency, and environmental responsibility, Arc Ciel empowers organizations in diverse sectors—from agriculture and renewable energy to education and conservation.

Founded on the belief that technology can be a powerful catalyst for positive change, Arc Ciel Africa brings state-of-the-art expertise in drone technology to address Africa's unique challenges. Our team of skilled professionals provides end-to-end drone solutions, including consultation, operations, maintenance, and certification, ensuring clients can seamlessly integrate UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) technology into their workflows. Our services are designed to improve productivity, reduce operational costs, and foster sustainable practices across industries.

In addition to our work with drones, Arc Ciel is committed to creating a greener, more efficient energy landscape. Through our solar system sizing and drone-based solar plant inspections, we help businesses optimize their energy output, maximize efficiency, and reduce their environmental footprint. We believe that sustainability is essential for Africa's future and strive to make renewable energy accessible and efficient for all.
